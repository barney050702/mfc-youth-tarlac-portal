# 🌐 MFC Youth Tarlac Chapter Portal — Free Online Publishing Guide

Your portal is built as a **self-contained Progressive Web App (PWA)** using pure HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Because it doesn't require a heavy backend server, you can host it online **100% FREE** with high speed and HTTPS security.

---

## Option 1: Netlify Drop (⚡ Fastest — Takes 30 Seconds!)

No command line required. Simply drag and drop your folder:

1. Open your web browser and go to: **https://app.netlify.com/drop**
2. Open Windows File Explorer to your project folder:
   `C:\Users\barne\.gemini\antigravity-ide\scratch\attendance-activity-portal\`
3. **Drag and drop** the entire `attendance-activity-portal` folder onto the Netlify Drop area.
4. Within 5 seconds, your site will be live!
5. *(Optional)* Create a free Netlify account to customize your URL to something like:
   👉 `https://mfcyouth-tarlac.netlify.app`

---

## Option 2: GitHub Pages (Recommended for Permanent Production Use)

1. Create a free account at **https://github.com**.
2. Click **New Repository** and name it `mfc-youth-tarlac-portal`. Make it **Public**.
3. Click **uploading an existing file** on the repository page and drag all 5 files:
   - `index.html`
   - `styles.css`
   - `script.js`
   - `manifest.json`
   - `mfc-logo.png`
4. Click **Commit changes**.
5. Go to repository **Settings → Pages**.
6. Under **Branch**, select `main` and `/ (root)` and save.
7. Your live public portal will be accessible at:
   👉 `https://yourusername.github.io/mfc-youth-tarlac-portal/`

---

## Option 3: Cloudflare Pages or Vercel

You can also drag and drop this folder or connect your GitHub repository to **Vercel** (`vercel.com`) or **Cloudflare Pages** (`pages.cloudflare.com`) for free global CDN hosting.

---

### 💡 PWA Mobile App Installation
Once published online, any Chapter Leader can visit your public URL on their phone (Chrome, Safari, or Edge) and tap **"Add to Home Screen"** to install it as an app with custom MFC Youth icons!
